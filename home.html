About KPF Foundation


KPF is the brain-child of a great visinory, Late Kedar Prasad. He always believed in giving back to the society in whatever small ways possible. He emphasised on the need to uplift the ones who haven't been as fortunate and the best way to do so is to empower them.


Currently as part of the foundation activities, Krishna Kumar Maddheshiya, is single-handedly taking his mission forward. Starting small, he has initiated a couple of improvements at his government school, like introducing the small kids(Future of our Nation) to digital literacy, designing revolutionary methods to retain them to higher education.

Lets take India forward...If there are times when you feel that your high earning job doesn't provide you a purposeful life, Come join us!!!

"A Life Led For Others Is Selfless And Worth Living!!!"


How Do You become A Member?


Step 1: Register With Our Foundation.


Step 2: Choose one of the following:

  • For Education - Choose a group of student and volunteer their education online, depending on your availability.
  • For Health - You can send us medicines or sanitary equipments online.
  • For Empowerment - You can choose a group of students from our school and provide them with an internship experience with your organisation.

Step 3: In case you are not able to contribute in person, you can sponser one of the student and give meaning to their life.


Step 4: Feel the joy of giving by enjoying beenifts from our foundation.
